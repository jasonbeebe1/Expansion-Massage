jason beebe has a masters degree in physics, over a decade of yoga practice, and has completed a 200 hour yoga teacher training program at sacred thread yoga here in atlanta. yoga plays a huge role in his approach to massage. the energy he brings to each session is grounded and mindfull. the name jason means "healer" in greek, and healing is his goal for his clients. he hopes to achieve this by applying massage techniques proven effective through scientific methodology while relying on the yogic tradition of tantra to guide him energeticly. tantra can be translated as "expansion-device," and this is the orgin of the name he chose for his practice. tantra teaches us to tune into our energy, cleanse and strengthen it, and increase our capacity to hold and direct it. and, just to be clear, jason does not practice "tantric massage" in the popular sense of "erotic massage." tantra celebrates every aspect of life. it is about awakening and amplifying the energy within, allowing it to flow freely and harmoniously. it lines up with other passions of his like existential philosophy and natural health. jason is currently finishing up massage school at the elaine sterling institute and hopes to be licensed to practice before the fall. until then he can't charge for services, so, if you have read this far, reward yourself and reach out for a free service that will count towards his training.
